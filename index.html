The Journey Unfolds

From historic Manila streets to cutting-edge tech offices, from mountain air to industry insights - eight days that transformed our perspective on Computer Science careers.

Day 1 - June 8 • Manila Arrival & Historic Discovery

8:25 AM arrival at NAIA Terminal 3 - stepping into Manila with excitement and anticipation. Our journey began with connecting to Philippine heritage through the Intramuros Fort Santiago Walking Tour, where we walked through centuries-old stone walls and battlements where national heroes like Dr. José Rizal were once imprisoned. The experience grounded us in our nation's history before diving into its technological future.

A peaceful afternoon at Luneta Park (Rizal Park) provided the perfect transition - strolling through manicured gardens, viewing historical monuments, and appreciating the green space that serves as Manila's cultural heart. The park offered quiet moments for reflection and group bonding.

Evening exploration of the massive SM Mall of Asia introduced us to Manila's scale and energy. As one of the world's largest malls, it showcased the Philippines' consumer culture and gave us our first taste of the city's vibrant commercial landscape. The sheer size helped us understand Manila as a major economic hub in Southeast Asia.

Day 2 - June 9 • Corporate Immersion Begins

Early start to Pasig City for our first corporate visit at Foundever Philippines (formerly Sitel Group). This global CX leader serving 45+ languages showed us how AI, cloud computing, and data analytics transform customer service. Interactive sessions with their operations and IT teams revealed insights into customer journey mapping, digital transformation, and cybersecurity in large-scale operations.

IBPAP Industry Briefing: Ms. Zoe Diaz De Rivera provided eye-opening statistics - the IT-BPM sector employs 1.7 million Filipinos and drives innovation through automation, AI, and RPA. Key takeaway: technical skills get you noticed, but communication, critical thinking, and adaptability get you hired.

Afternoon at Google Philippines BGC with Atty. Yves Gonzalez (Head of Government Affairs & Public Policy) was transformative. Learned about Google's AI evolution, digital inclusion initiatives like "Be Internet Awesome," and the critical importance of ethical tech governance. The office tour showcased innovation-driven workplace design that sparked our imagination about future tech careers.

Day 3 - June 10 • Skills Development & AI Innovation

Morning at Teleperformance Philippines (SM Aura BGC) with Ms. Beatrice Grace Bonjibod revealed TP's evolution beyond traditional customer support into digitally integrated business services. Key insight: 53% of their workforce are women, showcasing their commitment to gender equality. The Innovation and Solutions Hub tour demonstrated how automation and analytics shape modern business operations. Mock interviews with HR provided practical feedback crucial for job applications.

Afternoon at Kollab Philippines (Greenfield Tower, Mandaluyong) was transformative. Ralph Vincent Regalado, Chief Scientist and Head of AI, welcomed us with insights into collaborative technology solutions. Jazmine Calma taught us "Why Culture Matters" for product excellence. Toni-Jan Keith Monserrat's "Applied AI: From Classroom to Real Use Cases" connected our theoretical knowledge to real-world applications in agriculture, customer service, and health. Miguel Siriban's portfolio workshop gave actionable tips on showcasing projects and storytelling through design.

Day 4 - June 11 • Development Impact & Professional Branding

Morning at the Asian Development Bank revealed how technology serves humanitarian missions across Asia. Their emphasis on cybersecurity showed us that in fintech and international development, one security breach could affect millions of lives. Afternoon at Microsourcing in Eastwood was practical career preparation - learning LinkedIn optimization strategies, professional photo guidelines, and how to craft compelling professional summaries that attract recruiters in the competitive tech industry.

📸 Professional development: ADB mission insights and Microsourcing LinkedIn mastery
Day 5 - June 12 • Tagaytay Adventure

Break from corporate life! Journey to Tagaytay started with light rain at People's Park in the Sky - but the misty views of Taal Lake were magical. By the time we reached Sky Ranch, the rain had stopped and the real fun began. The Viking ride was absolutely thrilling - that moment of weightlessness mixed with pure terror and excitement! Sometimes the best team bonding happens when you're all screaming together on a ride.

📸 Adventure day: Misty Taal Lake views and Sky Ranch thrills (including the famous Viking ride!)
Day 6 - June 13 • Baguio City Exploration

1 AM departure for the City of Pines! Early morning visit to Benguet Strawberry Farm - fresh mountain air and sweet strawberries were the perfect start. The serene Chinese Bell Church provided peaceful moments before the action-packed day. Philippine Military Academy tour showed us discipline and excellence in action. Mines View Park offered breathtaking mountain vistas, while The Mansion showcased colonial architecture. The day's highlight: Burnham Park boat riding - navigating the lake with friends, lots of laughter, and a few near-collisions that made it even more memorable!

📸 Baguio exploration: From strawberry picking to boat adventures with the squad
Day 7 - June 14 • Free Time & Pasalubong Shopping

Free morning to explore Baguio at our own pace. The walk to SM Baguio was longer than expected but worth it for quality time with batch mates. Pasalubong shopping was serious business - bringing home jars of authentic Baguio strawberry jam and ube delicacies for family and friends. These sweet treats would later remind us of mountain adventures and friendship moments.

📸 Final Baguio day: SM exploration and bringing home sweet memories (literally!)
Day 8 - June 15 • Journey Home

Final breakfast at DJM Dormitory before departure. The 12:00 noon flight marked the end of our transformative journey, but the beginning of applying everything we learned. Carrying bags full of pasalubong and hearts full of memories, we returned home as changed individuals - more confident about our CS careers and grateful for the friendships forged during this incredible week.

📸 Farewell: Last group moments and carrying memories (and strawberry jam) home

Key Takeaways

Eight days that fundamentally reshaped our understanding of the tech industry and our future careers in Computer Science.

🤖

AI is Inevitable

Every single company emphasized AI and Machine Learning as the future. From neural networks to NLP applications, the message was crystal clear: we must focus intensively on these rapidly evolving technologies or risk being left behind.

🌐

Professional Networks Matter

Meeting industry leaders like Ralph Vincent Regalado opened our eyes to the power of professional connections. These relationships provide mentorship, opportunities, and insider insights into industry trends.

💼

Soft Skills = Hard Requirements

Technical skills get you noticed, but communication, presentation abilities, and interview confidence get you hired. Professional branding and LinkedIn optimization are now essential career tools.

🏢

Culture Over Compensation

Learning to identify healthy company cultures and growth-oriented environments is crucial. The right workplace culture can accelerate your career more than a higher starting salary.

🔒

Security Underpins Everything

From ADB's international operations to Google's global platforms, cybersecurity isn't optional - it's the foundation that makes digital innovation possible and trustworthy.

🚀

Continuous Learning is Survival

Technology evolution never stops. Staying updated with current trends, learning new frameworks, and adapting to emerging technologies isn't just for growth - it's for relevance.
